DUBAI DESERT SAFARI

Whether you have a stopover in Dubai or you are on a holiday or if you want to organize an event in the Desert, We can help you with all your demands. Desert Safari is probably one of the most authentic experiences Dubai can offer, so you must not miss it when you visit Dubai!

IMPORTANT NOTES / CANCELLATION / AMENDMENT / REFUND POLICY:

  • Booking must be within or more than 48 hours in advance.
  • Trip Cancellation one day before the tour date, 15% Cancellation Charges will apply.
  • Trip cancellation on the same day of the tour date, 100% Cancellation Charges will apply.
  • Trip amendment/rescheduling 48 hours before tour date; No Cancellation Charges will apply.
  • Pregnant women, people with back issues, senior citizens, or with heart problems are not suggested to do the Dune Bushing / Desert Safari. Make sure to notify us / driver to drop you directly to the camp area.
  • Pick up and Drop off on selected areas in within Dubai city limit
